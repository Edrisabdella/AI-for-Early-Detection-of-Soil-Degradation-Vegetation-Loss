Project Overview

TerraGuard is an open-source, AI-powered platform designed to tackle the silent crisis of land degradation through early detection and proactive intervention.

By leveraging cutting-edge Machine Learning, GIS, and Remote Sensing technologies, we identify the earliest signs of soil degradation and vegetation loss, enabling stakeholders to take preventive measures before damage becomes irreversible.

3.2B+

The Global Challenge

Causes of Land Degradation

  • Deforestation & land clearing
  • Unsustainable agriculture
  • Overgrazing & soil compaction
  • Climate change effects
  • Urban expansion

Effects & Impacts

  • Reduced agricultural productivity
  • Loss of biodiversity
  • Increased poverty & food insecurity
  • Water scarcity & quality issues
  • Climate change acceleration

East Africa Focus

  • Ethiopia: 50% of cultivated land degraded
  • Vulnerable soil types: Vertisols, Nitisols
  • Climate stressors: Droughts, erratic rainfall
  • Topography: Steep slopes increase erosion risk

Our Solution

Satellite Monitoring

Continuous monitoring using Sentinel-2, Landsat, and other satellite data sources to detect subtle changes in land health.

AI-Powered Analysis

Advanced machine learning models analyze multiple vegetation and soil indices to calculate our proprietary Land Health Index (LHI).

Early Warning System

Automated alerts notify stakeholders when early signs of degradation are detected, enabling timely intervention.

Interactive Dashboard

User-friendly interface for visualizing land health metrics, historical trends, and risk zones with actionable insights.
